The Exercise

The First

Pick any chord — Cm7, Gsus4, Bbmaj7, anything. Find it through all three shapes.

The Second

Pick a key. Pick a shape. Work through every chord quality in that key using that shape. Major, minor, 7, Maj7, m7, sus4. Understand what you are changing with each modification. Then pick again.

The 12 keys are listed below. Pick one at random. The randomness is the point. Every combination is valid. Every repetition builds fluency.

Come back to this exercise for the rest of your playing life.
