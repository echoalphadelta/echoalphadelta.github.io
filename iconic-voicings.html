Iconic Voicings

It is helpful to have chord voicings and grips we can refer to with names — names that are not pitch related. Here are some handy chords that have memorable non-pitch names. We can even lend them colours too.

The Power Chord

A dyad that lives at the bottom of each of our three grips. Here is a blue, red, and green power chord.

The Hendrix Chord — 7#9

Hendrix blew our minds with this one. He famously used it on E — here it is in D at the 5th position. Pull it out to put an exclamation on things.

D7#9

The James Brown Chord — 9

James Brown rocked the dance floor with this one — and you can too. G root at the 10th position.

G9

The James Bond Chord — minMaj9

Pull this one off to be cheeky. Dark, cinematic and unmistakeable.

EminMaj9
